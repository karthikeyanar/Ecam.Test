About Balkrishna Ind

Balkrishna Ind is in the Tyres sector. The current market capitalisation stands at Rs 23,350.30 crore.

The company management includes Arvind Poddar - Chairman & Managing Director, Rajiv Poddar - Joint Managing Director, Vipul Shah - Whole Time Director & Co. Secretary, Vijaylaxmi Poddar - Director, Sachin Nath Chaturvedi - Director, Khurshed Doongaji - Director, Laxmidas Merchant - Director, Sanjay Asher - Director, Ashok Saraf - Director.

It is listed on the BSE with a BSE Code of 502355 and the NSE with an NSE Code of BALKRISIND.

Its Registered office is at B-66, Waluj MIDC, Waluj Industrial Area,, Aurangabad,Maharashtra - 431136.

Their Registrars are Karvy Computershare Private Ltd.
